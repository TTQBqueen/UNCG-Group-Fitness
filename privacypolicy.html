Privacy Policy

Effective 2024

At Spartan Motion, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform to track physical activity, connect with other users, and set fitness goals. By using Spartan Motion, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, username, and any other details you provide when signing up for the app.
  • Fitness Data: Data related to your physical activity, goals, progress, and classes completed within the app.
  • Interaction Data: Information about your interactions with classes, such as reviews, sign-ups, and completed activities.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Spartan Motion platform, ensuring you can track your fitness progress.
  • Send notifications and updates about your progress, activities, and relevant app features.
  • Analyze user data to improve app performance, functionality, and user experience.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • If required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Spartan Motion and its users.

4. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of online storage or data transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting our support team.
  • Control the privacy settings for your profile and interactions with other users.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@spartanmotion.com.
