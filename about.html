About Spartan Motion

Spartan Motion is a platform that was created to help students exercise and stay fit without the stress of worrying about schedule conflicts.

Our application is designed for UNCG students specifically, so they can connect with different fitness instructors at UNCG.

Spartan Motion allows students who have completed fitness classes to write a review upon completeion. For more information about our comment guides, please visit our Privacy Policy page.
